# Nghi dinh 57/2022/ND-CP

**Van ban goc:** `data/landing/legal/nghi-dinh-57-2022.pdf`
**Nguon tai lieu:** Cong Bao Chinh phu, Nghi dinh so 57/2022/ND-CP
**Loai tai lieu:** legal

Nghi dinh 57/2022/ND-CP quy dinh cac danh muc chat ma tuy va tien chat. Van ban ban hanh phu luc cac danh muc, bao gom nhom chat ma tuy tuyet doi cam su dung trong y hoc va doi song xa hoi, nhom chat ma tuy duoc su dung han che trong phan tich, kiem nghiem, nghien cuu khoa hoc, dieu tra toi pham hoac linh vuc y te theo quy dinh, va cac danh muc tien chat can kiem soat.

Trong he thong RAG, tai lieu nay nen duoc truy xuat khi cau hoi nhac den danh muc chat ma tuy, tien chat, heroin, cocaine, methamphetamine, MDMA, can sa, tien chat thiet yeu, hoac viec phan loai chat cam. Nghi dinh 57 khong phai van ban quy dinh hinh phat; neu nguoi dung hoi ve muc phat, pipeline can ket hop voi Bo luat Hinh su hoac tai lieu phap ly lien quan khac.

Khi tra loi, can canh bao rang danh muc chat ma tuy co the duoc sua doi, bo sung boi van ban moi hon, vi vay cac truy van yeu cau "moi nhat" nen kiem tra them van ban cap nhat sau Nghi dinh 57/2022/ND-CP.
