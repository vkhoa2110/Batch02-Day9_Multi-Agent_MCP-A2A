# Bo luat Hinh su 2015, sua doi 2017 - Cac toi pham ve ma tuy

**Van ban goc:** `data/landing/legal/bo-luat-hinh-su-2015.pdf`
**Nguon tai lieu:** Cong Bao Chinh phu, Bo luat Hinh su so 100/2015/QH13 va Luat 12/2017/QH14 sua doi, bo sung
**URL tham chieu:** https://congbao.chinhphu.vn/noi-dung-van-ban-so-100-2015-qh13-18442 ; https://congbao.chinhphu.vn/van-ban/luat-so-12-2017-qh14-24289.htm
**Loai tai lieu:** legal

Chuong XX cua Bo luat Hinh su quy dinh nhom toi pham ve ma tuy. Cac dieu thuong duoc truy van gom: Dieu 247 ve toi trong cay thuoc phien, cay coca, cay can sa hoac cay khac co chua chat ma tuy; Dieu 248 ve toi san xuat trai phep chat ma tuy; Dieu 249 ve toi tang tru trai phep chat ma tuy; Dieu 250 ve toi van chuyen trai phep chat ma tuy; Dieu 251 ve toi mua ban trai phep chat ma tuy; Dieu 252 ve toi chiem doat chat ma tuy; Dieu 253 ve tien chat dung vao viec san xuat trai phep chat ma tuy; Dieu 254 ve phuong tien, dung cu dung vao viec san xuat hoac su dung trai phep chat ma tuy; Dieu 255 ve toi to chuc su dung trai phep chat ma tuy; Dieu 256 ve toi chua chap viec su dung trai phep chat ma tuy; Dieu 257 ve toi cuong buc nguoi khac su dung trai phep chat ma tuy; Dieu 258 ve toi loi keo nguoi khac su dung trai phep chat ma tuy; va Dieu 259 ve toi vi pham quy dinh ve quan ly chat ma tuy, tien chat, thuoc gay nghien, thuoc huong than.

Dieu 249 ve tang tru trai phep chat ma tuy la can cu chinh khi nguoi dung hoi "tang tru ma tuy bi phat the nao". Khung co ban cua toi nay la phat tu tu 01 nam den 05 nam neu hanh vi va khoi luong chat ma tuy thuoc truong hop luat dinh. Cac khung tang nang co the len 05-10 nam, 10-15 nam, hoac 15-20 nam tu tuy loai chat, khoi luong, so lan pham toi, tinh tiet co to chuc, tai pham nguy hiem, loi dung chuc vu quyen han, hoac su dung nguoi duoi 16 tuoi. Ngoai hinh phat tu, nguoi pham toi co the bi phat tien bo sung va bi cam dam nhiem chuc vu, cam hanh nghe hoac lam cong viec nhat dinh theo quy dinh.

Dieu 251 ve mua ban trai phep chat ma tuy co khung hinh phat nghiem khac hon hanh vi tang tru. Khung co ban la phat tu tu 02 nam den 07 nam. Neu co tinh tiet tang nang hoac khoi luong chat ma tuy lon, khung hinh phat co the tang len 07-15 nam, 15-20 nam, 20 nam, tu chung than hoac tu hinh tuy truong hop. Khi retrieval gap cau hoi ve "ban ma tuy", "mua ban ma tuy", "duong day mua ban", nen uu tien Dieu 251 hon Dieu 249.

Dieu 255 ve to chuc su dung trai phep chat ma tuy ap dung khi hanh vi khong chi la ca nhan su dung ma tuy ma con sap xep, chuan bi, lôi keo, cung cap dia diem, dung cu hoac dieu kien de nguoi khac su dung trai phep chat ma tuy. Day la dieu thuong lien quan den cac vu viec bao chi neu "to chuc su dung trai phep chat ma tuy". Khung hinh phat co ban la tu 02 nam den 07 nam va co the tang nang neu pham toi nhieu lan, doi voi nhieu nguoi, doi voi nguoi duoi 16 tuoi, gay ton hai suc khoe, gay chet nguoi hoac co tinh tiet nghiem trong khac.

Khi tra loi phap ly, pipeline can phan biet ro ba nhom hanh vi: tang tru la nam giu trai phep chat ma tuy; van chuyen la di chuyen trai phep chat ma tuy; mua ban la trao doi, ban, mua hoac moi gioi mua ban trai phep chat ma tuy. Neu nguoi dung hoi ve danh muc chat va tien chat, nen ket hop voi Nghi dinh 57/2022/ND-CP. Neu nguoi dung hoi ve cai nghien, quan ly nguoi su dung trai phep chat ma tuy, nen ket hop voi Luat Phong, chong ma tuy 2021 va Nghi dinh 105/2021/ND-CP.
