# Luat Phong, chong ma tuy 2021

**Van ban goc:** `data/landing/legal/luat-phong-chong-ma-tuy-2021.pdf`
**Nguon tai lieu:** Cong Bao Chinh phu, Luat so 73/2021/QH14
**Loai tai lieu:** legal

Luat Phong, chong ma tuy nam 2021 quy dinh ve phong ngua, ngan chan, dau tranh chong toi pham va te nan ma tuy; kiem soat cac hoat dong hop phap lien quan den ma tuy; quan ly nguoi su dung trai phep chat ma tuy; cai nghien ma tuy; trach nhiem cua ca nhan, gia dinh, co quan, to chuc trong phong, chong ma tuy.

Van ban nay la nen tang phap ly chinh cho cac cau hoi ve khai niem chat ma tuy, tien chat, nguoi su dung trai phep chat ma tuy, trach nhiem phong chong ma tuy trong cong dong va cac bien phap quan ly, giao duc, cai nghien. Khi tra loi cau hoi phap ly, can phan biet giua quy dinh chung cua Luat Phong, chong ma tuy va cac toi danh/hinh phat cu the trong Bo luat Hinh su.

Mot so chu de thuong gap: trach nhiem tuyen truyen phong chong ma tuy; quan ly nguoi su dung trai phep chat ma tuy; lap ho so cai nghien; cai nghien tu nguyen, cai nghien bat buoc; kiem soat tien chat va thuoc thu y, thuoc tan duoc co chua chat ma tuy; hop tac quoc te trong phong chong ma tuy.
