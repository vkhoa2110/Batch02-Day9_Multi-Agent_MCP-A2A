# Bat giam dien vien Huu Tin vi lien quan ma tuy

**Source:** Bao Nguoi Lao Dong
**URL:** https://nld.com.vn/phap-luat/bat-giam-dien-vien-huu-tin-vi-lien-quan-ma-tuy-20220617202258452.htm
**Crawled:** 2026-06-08T14:45:00+07:00
**Type:** news

Bai bao thong tin Cong an quan 8, TP.HCM bat tam giam dien vien Huu Tin va mot nguoi khac do lien quan den hanh vi to chuc, tang tru va su dung trai phep chat ma tuy. Theo phan tom tat noi dung, luc kiem tra can ho, luc luong chuc nang phat hien mot so vat chung nghi la ma tuy va nhieu nguoi co mat trong phong. Bai viet cung de cap viec co quan dieu tra tiep tuc lam ro vai tro cua tung nguoi trong vu viec.

Day la mot nguon tin phu hop cho truy van ve nghe si Viet Nam lien quan ma tuy, dac biet voi cac cau hoi ve dien vien Huu Tin, su kien nam 2022, hanh vi bi dieu tra va boi canh phap ly. Khi dua vao cau tra loi RAG, can ghi ro day la thong tin bao chi dua theo co quan cong an tai thoi diem dang tin, khong mo rong thanh ket luan ngoai pham vi bai bao.
