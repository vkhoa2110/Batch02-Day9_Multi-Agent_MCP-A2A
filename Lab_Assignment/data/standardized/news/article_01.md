# Cong an TP.HCM thong tin vu ca si Chi Dan, nguoi mau Andrea Aybar bi bat

**Source:** VOV
**URL:** https://vov.vn/phap-luat/cong-an-tphcm-thong-tin-vu-ca-si-chi-dan-nguoi-mau-andrea-aybar-bi-bat-post1135460.vov
**Crawled:** 2026-06-08T14:45:00+07:00
**Type:** news

Bai viet cua VOV tuong thuat thong tin Cong an TP.HCM cong bo ngay 14/11/2024 ve viec ca si Chi Dan va nguoi mau Andrea Aybar (An Tay) bi bat de dieu tra lien quan hanh vi to chuc su dung trai phep chat ma tuy. Vu viec duoc dat trong qua trinh mo rong chuyen an VN10 ve duong day van chuyen ma tuy tu Phap ve Viet Nam qua san bay Tan Son Nhat. Theo noi dung bai bao, co quan cong an cho biet qua qua trinh lan theo dong chay ma tuy, luc luong chuc nang da tiep tuc triet pha nhieu duong day, bang nhom mua ban va van chuyen trai phep chat ma tuy.

Noi dung nay phu hop voi corpus RAG vi no cung cap boi canh ve viec nguoi noi tieng bi dieu tra, toi danh duoc nhac den, co quan cong bo thong tin va moi lien he voi chuyen an lon. Khi tra loi, he thong can dien dat than trong: cac thong tin ve ca nhan can gan voi nguon bao chi va trang thai to tung tai thoi diem bai viet, tranh suy dien vuot qua noi dung da cong bo.
