# Nha thiet ke Nguyen Cong Tri bi bat vi lien quan ma tuy

**Source:** VnExpress
**URL:** https://vnexpress.net/nha-thiet-ke-nguyen-cong-tri-bi-bat-vi-lien-quan-ma-tuy-4917929.html
**Crawled:** 2026-06-08T14:45:00+07:00
**Type:** news

Bai viet cua VnExpress dang ngay 23/7/2025 dua tin nha thiet ke Nguyen Cong Tri bi bat qua tang su dung ma tuy tai nha rieng trong qua trinh canh sat dieu tra mot duong day mua ban chat cam. Bai viet cung neu mot so thong tin nghe nghiep, tam anh huong trong linh vuc thoi trang va boi canh co quan cong an mo rong dieu tra duong day lien quan. Day la nguon tin gan thoi diem hien dai hon trong corpus, phu hop voi cac cau hoi ve nguoi noi tieng trong linh vuc thoi trang lien quan ma tuy.

Khi su dung trong he thong RAG, can trinh bay day la thong tin theo VnExpress va gan voi ngay dang bai. Neu cau hoi yeu cau trang thai phap ly moi nhat, he thong nen khuyen nghi kiem tra nguon cap nhap moi hon vi to tung co the thay doi sau ngay bai bao duoc cong bo.
